The Dialectic of Enlightenment

Gretel helped Theodor Adorno and Max Horkheimer create the manuscript for The Dialectic of Enlightenment, and is acknowledged in the foreword. She took notes during discussions between Adorno and Horkheimer, which then developed into the book.

Published in 1947, The Dialectic of Enlightenment is a core text of critical theory. It examines the failure of Enlightenment, and the rise of fascism and totalitarianism.
