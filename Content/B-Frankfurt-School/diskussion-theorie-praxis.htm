Diskussion über Theorie und Praxis

As with The Dialectic of Enlightenment, Gretel acted as stenographer, recording conversations between her husband and Max Horkheimer.

Originally created in 1956, and reissued in 2011 as Towards a New Manifesto, Diskussion über Theorie und Praxis was intended to be contemporary version of Mark and Engel's The Communist Manifesto.
