Aesthetic Theory

Unlike The Dialectic of Enlightenment and Diskussion über Theorie und Praxis, Gretel had a much clearer editorial role in the creation of Aesthetic Theory. Working with Rolf Tiedemann, using Adorno's drafts, Gretel assembled and completed the book.

Aesthetic Theory is Theodor Adorno's last work, published posthumously in 1970. It examines the evolution of art and the causes of its current status within capitalism and modernity.
